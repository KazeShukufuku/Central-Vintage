## Assets

The current `pickling_guide.png` item and GUI textures are blank development placeholders.
They should be replaced with original assets before public release.

Final public-release assets should be original work made for this project. They may reference the general Cooking Guide layout and automation concept from Create: Central Kitchen, as well as the general themes of pickling automation, such as fermenting jars, brine, salt, glass, labels, pickled peppers, and the muted teal brine color `#528185`, but should not be traced from or directly edited from Create: Central Kitchen or Vintage Delight assets.
